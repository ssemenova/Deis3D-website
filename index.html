3D Printing at Brandeis University


We aim to provide resources and cutting edge technology to the Brandeis community that allows them to innovate and create.
We promote engineering and robotics, beta-test 3D printers, compete in intercollegiate events and design/print challenges, host various inter-club events, 3D model using Cad software, host speaker events, build 3D printers, and do 3D scanning.
We also run the Printathon, a bi-yearly hackathon for 3D printing.

Come to our weekly meetings!

When: Every Wednesday from 7:30 - 8:30 PM
Where: MakerLab, Brandeis University Library
Who: You!
